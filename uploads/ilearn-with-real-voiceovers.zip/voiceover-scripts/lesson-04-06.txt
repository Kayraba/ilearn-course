When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Staff will name letters from the your name or a familiar word. You point first, then presses. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Use simple words such as cat, bus and tea. After each word, press Space before the next word. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type the word: cat. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Practise holding Shift and pressing one letter. Keep this calm; it is a stretch task. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
