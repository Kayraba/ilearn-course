When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: You open Word, clicks the page, and watches for the flashing cursor. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Build one sentence together: capital letter, words, spaces and full stop. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type a full sentence. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Press Enter and add another sentence. Staff supports punctuation only after the idea is typed. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
