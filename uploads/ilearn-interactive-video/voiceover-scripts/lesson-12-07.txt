When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Show a plain sentence and a title. Ask which one is easier to spot and why. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Type a useful title, select it, make it bigger, make it bold and centre it. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type a title for a simple document. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Use alignment buttons to show how text moves. Return the final title to centre. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
