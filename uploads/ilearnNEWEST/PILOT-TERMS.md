# Pilot Terms - Draft

iLearn is provided as a controlled pilot version for evaluation and supported
delivery. It is not yet a fully certified production system.

## Intended use

- supported digital-skills learning
- staff-led or staff-supported sessions
- controlled client/council pilot
- progress reporting and certificate evidence

## Not intended for

- unsupervised safeguarding-critical decisions
- storing unnecessary sensitive information
- public self-registration without staff/client oversight
- full production rollout without further review

## Operator responsibilities

The operator/client should confirm:

- data protection contact
- privacy notice
- retention period
- safeguarding process
- staff onboarding
- database backup plan
- support route for incidents

## Pilot success measures

- learner engagement
- lesson completion
- staff usability
- accessibility feedback
- reporting usefulness
- support required per learner
