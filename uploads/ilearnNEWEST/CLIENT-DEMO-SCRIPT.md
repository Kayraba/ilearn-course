# Client Demo Script

Use this for a short professional walkthrough.

## Opening

"iLearn is an accessible digital-skills platform for supported adults. The aim is
to build independence through small, repeatable computer tasks while giving staff
evidence of progress, support level and consent records."

## Learner experience

"Learners do not need email accounts. They type their name on the start screen.
iLearn creates or reloads that learner profile, then keeps track of completed
lessons, activity progress and certificate status under that name."

"Each lesson has a clear goal, a short walkthrough, a practical activity and a
simple completion step. Staff can record whether the learner completed the task
independently, with verbal support, with a gesture prompt or with physical
support."

## Staff experience

"The staff dashboard shows enrolled learners, progress, recent activity and
certificates. Each learner record includes lesson history, prompt level evidence,
consent/capacity details and audit history."

## Closing

"The pilot is designed to be calm for learners and useful for staff. It gives the
service a structured course, safer sign-in and reporting evidence without making
the learner experience complicated."
