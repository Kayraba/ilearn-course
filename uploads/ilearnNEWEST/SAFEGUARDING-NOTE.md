# Safeguarding Note - Controlled Pilot

iLearn is a learning support tool. It does not replace safeguarding policies,
care plans or staff judgement.

## Staff responsibilities

Staff should:

- support learners at an appropriate pace
- avoid sharing personal learner information publicly
- record consent/capacity information before meaningful participation
- use respectful language in notes
- follow the client/operator safeguarding policy
- report concerns through the agreed service process

## Learner wellbeing

Lessons should stop or pause if a learner becomes distressed, tired or
overwhelmed. Completion is not more important than learner wellbeing.

## Data sensitivity

Consent/capacity notes may contain sensitive information. Keep notes factual,
necessary and respectful. Do not enter unnecessary medical or family detail.
