# Data Retention Policy - Pilot Draft

Status: draft. The operator/client must confirm retention before live use.

## Suggested pilot retention

Keep learner records only for the period needed to run and evaluate the pilot.

Default placeholder: `[e.g. delete or review 6 months after pilot end]`

## Data included

- learner profile name
- progress and completion records
- prompt/support levels
- certificate records
- consent/capacity notes
- audit logs
- staff/admin account records

## End of pilot

At the end of the pilot, the operator should decide whether to:

- delete learner records
- export reports for the client, then delete database records
- continue the service under a production agreement

## Backup retention

Database backups should follow the hosting provider and operator retention
policy. Confirm backup retention before real learner data is added.
