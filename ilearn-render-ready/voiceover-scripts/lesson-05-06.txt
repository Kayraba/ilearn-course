When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Point to minimise, maximise and close. Explain that X means finished with this window. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Open one app, wait, minimise it, bring it back, then close it. Repeat slowly. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type the word for the button that closes a window. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Staff gives the four actions in order. You attempts each before staff prompts. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
