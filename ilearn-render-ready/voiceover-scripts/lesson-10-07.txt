When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Practise selecting a word by double-clicking or dragging. Staff can use a large sample word. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Select one word, copy it with Ctrl + C, click lower on the page and paste with Ctrl + V. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type the shortcut letter for paste. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: You copies a name, pastes it twice, then undoes one paste. Staff only prompts when needed. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
