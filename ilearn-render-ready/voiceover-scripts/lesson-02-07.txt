When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Staff will ask you to move slowly to top-left, top-right, bottom-left and bottom-right. Repeat until movement is calmer. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Use real icons or safe blank desktop space. Staff will name a target, You moves to it and single-clicks once. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type the word click. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Staff will say the target name but avoids pointing. Move back to gesture prompts only if you gets stuck. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
