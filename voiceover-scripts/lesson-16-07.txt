When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Show that formulas are instructions for Excel. Say: equals means Excel, please work this out. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Type =2+3, press Enter, then change the numbers and try again. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type a formula to add 2 and 3. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Return to iLearn, mark the lesson complete, and open the certificate together. Staff will ask you what you are most proud of. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
