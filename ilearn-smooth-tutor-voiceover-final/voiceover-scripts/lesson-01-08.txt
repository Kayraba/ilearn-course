When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Check the device is charged, the keyboard and mouse are connected, and you can sit comfortably with the screen at a good distance. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Complete the full start-up routine twice. First time with staff prompts, second time with fewer prompts. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type a practice password to sign in. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Open the Start menu, find the power option, and identify Shut down. Staff can complete the final click if needed. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
