When you start the activities, they will follow the same order as this lesson. First, you do a warm-up: Pick something useful: snacks and prices, bus times, chores, attendance or weekly spending. Then you answer quick checks, so we know the key words make sense. Next, you practise the real computer skill: Create headings in row 1, then enter at least five rows of simple data. You may also type a short answer, for example: Type a useful table heading. At the end, you finish with a stretch or reflection: Enter simple prices and apply currency formatting. Discuss why money formatting helps. Take one activity at a time. If you need help, ask staff, then try the next step yourself.
